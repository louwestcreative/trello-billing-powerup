Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

Introduction

The Billing & Time Tracking Power-Up ("the Power-Up") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our Power-Up.

Data We Store

The Power-Up stores the following data in Trello:

  • Billing Data: Charges, payments, amounts, dates, and descriptions you manually enter
  • Toggl API Key: Your Toggl API key (stored at board level, encrypted by Trello)
  • Client Names: Optional client names you assign to cards

Data We Access

The Power-Up accesses:

  • Trello Data: Card names, labels, and basic board information
  • Toggl Data: Time entries, projects, and clients from your Toggl account (when configured)

How We Use Your Data

  • Display billing information on your Trello cards
  • Calculate balances and totals
  • Integrate with Toggl to show billable hours
  • All processing happens in your browser

Data Storage

  • All data is stored within Trello's infrastructure using Trello's storage APIs
  • We do not store any data on external servers
  • Data is only accessible to board members who have the Power-Up enabled

Third-Party Services

The Power-Up integrates with Toggl Track for time tracking. When you configure the Toggl integration:

  • Your Toggl API key is stored in Trello (not on our servers)
  • Time entries are fetched directly from Toggl's API
  • Please review Toggl's Privacy Policy

Data Security

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Your Toggl API key is stored securely by Trello
  • No billing data leaves your Trello account

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View all data stored by the Power-Up
  • Delete any billing records manually
  • Remove the Power-Up to delete all associated data
  • Revoke Toggl API access at any time

Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with the Power-Up:

  1. Remove the Power-Up from your board
  2. All stored charges, payments, and settings will be deleted
  3. Toggl data remains in your Toggl account unchanged

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
